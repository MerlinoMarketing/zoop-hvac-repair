# Duct Cleaning Services in Pompano Beach, FL

## Professional Air Duct Cleaning for Healthier Indoor Air

Your home's ductwork is the respiratory system of your HVAC. Over time, dust, pollen, pet dander, mold spores, and other contaminants accumulate inside your ducts. Every time your AC runs, these particles circulate through your living spaces. Professional duct cleaning removes these contaminants and restores clean airflow.

## Signs Your Ducts Need Cleaning

- **Visible dust blowing from vents** when the system kicks on
- **Musty or stale odors** when the AC is running
- **Increased allergy or asthma symptoms** at home
- **Excessive dust buildup** on furniture despite regular cleaning
- **Visible mold growth** near vents or inside ductwork
- **Pest evidence** (droppings, nesting materials) in or around ducts
- **Recently completed renovations** that generated construction dust
- **No record of previous duct cleaning** (or more than 3-5 years since last service)

## Our Duct Cleaning Process

### 1. Pre-Cleaning Inspection
We inspect your entire duct system using a camera to assess conditions, identify problem areas, and document the starting state of your ductwork.

### 2. System Protection
We protect your home's flooring, furniture, and finishes. Drop cloths are placed at every register location and around the air handler.

### 3. Source Removal Cleaning
We use the NADCA-recommended source removal method with high-powered vacuum equipment and mechanical agitation tools. This method physically removes contaminants rather than just loosening them.

### 4. Supply and Return Cleaning
Every supply duct, return duct, and trunk line is thoroughly cleaned. We access the system through existing registers and, when necessary, create small access points that are sealed after cleaning.

### 5. Air Handler Cleaning
The blower compartment, evaporator coil housing, and drain pan are cleaned as part of a comprehensive service. These components collect significant amounts of debris.

### 6. Sanitization (Optional)
For homes with mold concerns or odor issues, we apply an EPA-registered antimicrobial treatment to inhibit future microbial growth inside the ductwork.

### 7. Final Inspection and Report
We re-inspect the system with a camera to verify thorough cleaning and provide before/after documentation.

## Benefits of Professional Duct Cleaning

- **Improved Air Quality** - Remove allergens, dust, and contaminants from your breathing air
- **Better Efficiency** - Clean ducts allow unrestricted airflow, reducing energy consumption
- **Reduced Allergies** - Less pollen, dust mites, and pet dander circulating in your home
- **Odor Elimination** - Remove musty smells caused by mold, bacteria, or accumulated debris
- **Extended Equipment Life** - Clean ductwork reduces strain on your HVAC system
- **Healthier Home** - Particularly beneficial for households with children, elderly, or immunocompromised members

## How Often Should Ducts Be Cleaned?

The EPA recommends duct cleaning when there is visible mold, pest infestation, or excessive dust. For South Florida homes, we typically recommend cleaning every 3-5 years, or more frequently if you have pets, allergies, or recent construction.
