## Residential AC Repair vs. Replacement

Not sure if repair is the right choice? Here's how to decide:

### Choose Repair When:
- Roof is less than 15 years old
- Damage is localized to one area
- Less than 30% of roof is affected
- Roof structure is sound
- Repair cost is less than 30% of replacement cost

### Consider Replacement When:
- Roof is 20+ years old
- Multiple areas need repair
- More than 30% of shingles are damaged
- Roof decking shows rot or sagging
- You're planning to sell soon

**Not sure which is right?** Our free inspection includes an honest assessment. We'll recommend replacement only when repair doesn't make financial sense.
