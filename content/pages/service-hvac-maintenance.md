## What We Look For During Inspections

### Shingle Issues
- **Missing Shingles:** Wind damage, improper installation
- **Cracked Shingles:** Age, thermal expansion, improper installation
- **Curled Shingles:** Age, moisture, poor ventilation
- **Blistered Shingles:** Trapped moisture, manufacturing defect
- **Granule Loss:** Age, hail damage, defective shingles
- **Torn Shingles:** Wind damage, improper installation
- **Algae/Moss Growth:** Moisture retention, shade exposure

### Flashing Problems
- **Rusted Flashing:** Age, water exposure
- **Separated Flashing:** Thermal expansion, improper installation
- **Missing Flashing:** Installation error, emergency HVAC
- **Cracked Caulking:** Age, thermal cycling
- **Improper Installation:** Wrong type, incorrect technique

### Structural Concerns
- **Sagging:** Insufficient support, rot, snow load damage
- **Soft Spots:** Decking rot from water intrusion
- **Improper Ventilation:** Leads to moisture and heat damage
- **Fascia/Soffit Rot:** Water damage, age
- **Inadequate Support:** Undersized framing, removed supports

### Water Intrusion Signs
- **Ceiling Stains:** Active or past leaks
- **Attic Moisture:** Poor ventilation, leaks
- **Mold/Mildew:** Persistent moisture issues
- **Rot:** Long-term water exposure
- **Damaged Insulation:** Water saturation

### Installation Quality Issues
- **Improper Nailing:** Over/under driven, wrong placement
- **Missing Underlayment:** Installation shortcut
- **Incorrect Material:** Wrong products for application
- **Poor Workmanship:** Sloppy installation, shortcuts
- **Code Violations:** Non-compliant installation
