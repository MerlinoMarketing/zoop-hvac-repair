# HVAC Maintenance Services in Pompano Beach, FL

## Preventive Maintenance That Pays for Itself

In South Florida's relentless heat, your AC system works harder and longer than systems in most other parts of the country. Regular maintenance isn't optional here — it's essential. A well-maintained system runs more efficiently, breaks down less often, and lasts years longer than a neglected one.

## What's Included in Our Maintenance Visit

### Outdoor Unit (Condenser)
- Clean condenser coils and remove debris
- Check refrigerant charge and pressures
- Inspect contactors and electrical connections
- Test capacitors and measure amperage
- Lubricate fan motor bearings
- Check condenser fan blade condition
- Verify proper clearance around unit

### Indoor Unit (Air Handler / Evaporator)
- Clean or replace air filter
- Inspect evaporator coil condition
- Clear condensate drain line
- Test blower motor operation and amperage
- Check electrical connections and tighten terminals
- Inspect ductwork connections for leaks
- Verify supply and return airflow

### System Performance
- Measure temperature differential (supply vs. return)
- Check thermostat calibration and programming
- Test all safety controls and switches
- Inspect refrigerant lines and insulation
- Document system readings for comparison
- Provide written condition report

## Benefits of Regular Maintenance

- **Lower Energy Bills** - A clean, tuned system operates up to 15% more efficiently
- **Fewer Breakdowns** - Most emergency repairs are preventable with regular service
- **Extended Equipment Life** - Well-maintained systems last 3-5 years longer
- **Better Air Quality** - Clean filters and coils improve indoor air quality
- **Warranty Compliance** - Most manufacturers require annual maintenance to keep warranties valid
- **Peace of Mind** - Know your system is ready for the hottest days

## How Often Should You Schedule Maintenance?

For South Florida homes, we recommend at minimum one comprehensive tune-up per year, ideally in the spring before the peak cooling season. Systems older than 10 years or those running year-round benefit from twice-yearly service.
