# Indoor Air Quality Services in Pompano Beach, FL

## Breathe Cleaner, Healthier Air at Home

The EPA estimates that indoor air can be 2-5 times more polluted than outdoor air. In South Florida, where homes are sealed tight for energy efficiency, pollutants like mold spores, dust mites, pet dander, VOCs, and bacteria can accumulate to unhealthy levels. Our indoor air quality solutions address these problems at the source.

## Indoor Air Quality Solutions

### Whole-Home Air Purification
We install whole-home air purifiers that work with your existing HVAC system to remove up to 99.98% of airborne particles, including viruses, bacteria, mold spores, and allergens. These systems are far more effective than portable room units.

### UV Germicidal Lights
UV-C germicidal lamps installed inside your air handler kill mold, bacteria, and viruses as air passes through the system. They also keep your evaporator coil clean, improving efficiency and preventing musty odors.

### Advanced Filtration
We upgrade your filtration system with high-efficiency media filters or electronic air cleaners that capture particles standard filters miss. Options range from MERV 11 media filters to HEPA-level whole-home filtration.

### Humidity Control
South Florida's high humidity creates ideal conditions for mold growth and dust mite proliferation. We install whole-home dehumidifiers that work with your AC to maintain optimal humidity levels between 45-55%.

### Ventilation Solutions
Proper ventilation brings in fresh outdoor air while exhausting stale indoor air. Energy recovery ventilators (ERVs) provide fresh air without sacrificing energy efficiency — particularly important in tightly sealed homes.

## Signs of Poor Indoor Air Quality

- Persistent allergy or asthma symptoms at home
- Musty or stale odors
- Visible mold growth near vents or walls
- Excessive humidity or condensation on windows
- Frequent headaches or fatigue when at home
- Dust accumulation despite regular cleaning
