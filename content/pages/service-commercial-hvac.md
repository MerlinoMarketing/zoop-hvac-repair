## Commercial HVAC Systems We Install

### TPO Roofing (Thermoplastic Polyolefin)
TPO is the most popular commercial HVAC membrane, offering excellent durability and energy efficiency.

**Ideal For:**
- Office buildings
- Retail centers
- Warehouses
- Light manufacturing facilities

**Benefits:**
- Energy Star rated - reflects heat, reduces cooling costs
- Highly resistant to UV radiation and ozone
- Heat-welded seams create watertight bonds
- 20-30 year lifespan with proper maintenance
- Cost-effective installation

**Installation Cost:** $4-$8 per square foot

### EPDM Roofing (Rubber Membrane)
EPDM rubber roofing provides proven performance at an economical price point.

**Ideal For:**
- Budget-conscious projects
- Low-slope commercial buildings
- Schools and municipal buildings
- Storage facilities

**Benefits:**
- Proven 40+ year track record
- Excellent resistance to hail and weather
- Quick installation reduces labor costs
- Easy to repair and maintain
- Environmentally friendly - recyclable

**Installation Cost:** $3.50-$7 per square foot

### PVC Roofing (Polyvinyl Chloride)
Premium single-ply membrane offering superior chemical and fire resistance.

**Ideal For:**
- Restaurants with grease exposure
- Chemical processing facilities
- Buildings requiring Class A fire rating
- High-end commercial properties

**Benefits:**
- Superior chemical resistance
- Excellent fire resistance
- Highly reflective - maximum energy savings
- Extremely durable heat-welded seams
- 25-30+ year lifespan

**Installation Cost:** $5.50-$10 per square foot

### Built-Up Roofing (BUR)
Traditional multi-layer system with proven longevity.

**Ideal For:**
- Buildings requiring foot traffic roofs
- Heavy equipment installations on roof
- Buildings with HVAC units on roof
- Traditional commercial buildings

**Benefits:**
- Excellent waterproofing with multiple layers
- Handles foot traffic well
- Long lifespan - 20-30 years
- Proven technology with 100+ year history
- Good insulation properties

**Installation Cost:** $4-$9 per square foot

### Modified Bitumen Roofing
Hybrid system combining traditional built-up roofing with modern polymers.

**Ideal For:**
- Buildings in extreme climates
- Roofs with moderate foot traffic
- Cost-effective durability needs

**Benefits:**
- Excellent tear resistance
- Good performance in temperature extremes
- Easy to repair
- Multiple installation methods
- 15-20 year lifespan

**Installation Cost:** $3-$7 per square foot

### Metal Roofing
Standing seam and corrugated metal systems for long-term durability.

**Ideal For:**
- Manufacturing facilities
- Warehouses
- Agricultural buildings
- Steep-slope commercial buildings

**Benefits:**
- 40-60+ year lifespan
- Virtually maintenance-free
- Excellent weather resistance
- Highly energy efficient
- Sustainable - recyclable

**Installation Cost:** $6-$12 per square foot

## Commercial vs. Residential Roofing: Key Differences

### Structural Considerations
Commercial roofs are typically flat or low-slope, requiring different waterproofing approaches than steep residential roofs.

### Material Selection
Commercial roofing uses membranes (TPO, EPDM, PVC) and built-up systems designed for large expanses and low slopes.

### Installation Complexity
Commercial projects involve HVAC equipment, drainage systems, penetrations, and parapets requiring specialized expertise.

### Building Codes
Commercial buildings face stricter fire ratings, energy codes, and accessibility requirements.

### Project Scale
Commercial roofs are larger, requiring more planning, coordination, and specialized equipment.

### How much does commercial HVAC cost in [City]?
Commercial roofing in [City] typically costs $4-$10 per square foot installed, depending on the system selected. A 10,000 square foot roof ranges from $40,000-$100,000. Factors affecting cost include system type, roof accessibility, existing roof removal, and insulation requirements. [Brand Name] provides detailed estimates breaking down all costs.

### How long does a commercial roof last?
Commercial roof lifespan varies by system: TPO/PVC (20-30 years), EPDM (20-25 years), Built-up roofing (20-30 years), Modified bitumen (15-20 years), and Metal roofing (40-60+ years). Proper installation and regular maintenance significantly extend these lifespans.

### Do you work on occupied buildings?
Yes! Most of our commercial projects involve occupied buildings. We schedule work to minimize disruption, communicate with tenants and employees, maintain safety protocols, and can work during off-hours if needed.

### How long does commercial roof installation take?
A typical 10,000 square foot commercial roof takes 5-10 days to install, depending on system complexity, weather, and existing roof removal requirements. We provide detailed timelines in our proposals and keep you updated throughout the project.

### What commercial HVAC system is best?
The "best" system depends on your specific needs, budget, and building characteristics. TPO offers the best value for most applications, while PVC excels for restaurants and chemical exposure. EPDM is the most economical option. We'll recommend the optimal system during our assessment.

### Do you offer commercial roof maintenance?
Yes! [Brand Name] offers comprehensive maintenance programs including bi-annual inspections, minor repairs, drain cleaning, and condition reporting. Maintenance programs cost $500-$2,000 annually depending on roof size and extend roof life significantly.

### Will you work with my architect or engineer?
Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with architects, engineers, and general contractors. We can work from your specs or provide design-build services.

### Do you handle commercial roof warranties?
Yes. We offer 10-30 year warranties on commercial installations, including both materials and workmanship. We also facilitate manufacturer warranties and handle all warranty service needs.
