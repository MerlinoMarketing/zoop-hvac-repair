### Is the inspection and estimate really free?
Yes! All inspections and estimates are completely free with no obligation. We don't charge consultation fees or use high-pressure sales tactics. Our goal is providing honest assessments and competitive pricing - you decide if we're right for you.

### Do you offer emergency services?
Absolutely. Our 24/7 emergency line [Emergency Phone] connects you with on-call crews available for emergency tarping, leak mitigation, and emergency HVAC assessment. We typically arrive within [X] hours anywhere in our service area.

### How long does it take to get an estimate?
For simple repairs, we often provide estimates same-day after inspection. Complex projects or duct cleaning may take 24-48 hours for detailed, accurate estimates. We always explain what's included and answer all questions.

### Do you work with insurance companies?
Yes! We work with all major insurance companies and provide free claim assistance including damage documentation, adjuster meetings, and claim negotiation. We handle the paperwork while you focus on getting your home repaired.

### What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept cash, checks, all major credit cards, and offer financing options with approved credit including 0% interest plans. Insurance claim customers can assign payments directly to us in most cases.

### Do you offer warranties?
Yes. All installations include our [X]-year workmanship warranty covering labor and installation. Materials carry manufacturer warranties from 10-50 years depending on the product. We also offer extended warranty options for additional protection.

### Can you match competitors' prices?
We offer competitive pricing and a price-match guarantee on comparable quotes. If you receive a lower estimate from a licensed, insured contractor for the same scope and materials, we'll match or beat it.
